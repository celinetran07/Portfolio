Woodwork — May 2022

Oak Shelf

MediumOak Wood, Jigsaw, Sanding Machine, Oil Finish
DateMay 2022
TypeGift — Handmade

A shelf I made as a birthday gift for a close friend who had wanted one in her room. My mother's boyfriend owns various woodworking materials and tools which made this possible. I measured and cut a long piece of hardwood and two smaller side pieces using a jigsaw, then shaved and sanded the surfaces smooth. An oil finish brought out the natural colour and grain of the oak.
