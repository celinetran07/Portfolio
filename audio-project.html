Morning Shower Montage

Tools Adobe Audition, Audacity
Medium Field Recording, Audio Editing
Year 2024
Type Creative Audio

A sound montage assembled from recordings captured in a bathroom — water running, surfaces, echoes, the ambient noise of an everyday private space. The piece explores the sonic texture of a room that is rarely listened to deliberately, turning the mundane into something worth attending to.
