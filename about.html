About Me

Céline Tran

I'm a Computer Science and Computation Arts student at Concordia University in Montréal, expected to graduate in June 2026. My practice combines programming and design to build interactive digital experiences from websites and interfaces to 3D environments and games.

I work with JavaScript, React, Unreal Engine, Blender, and Figma. I treat interaction as a central design element, paying close attention to how users respond to structure, feedback, and constraints.

Download Résumé Download PDF

I create interactive digital projects that focus on how people engage with systems. My work includes websites and 3D environments where users are not just viewing content, but actively navigating, clicking, and making decisions. I am interested in how small interactions shape user behaviour and how digital spaces can guide, limit, or encourage certain actions. Rather than treating digital work as something passive, I design systems that feel dynamic and responsive where each interaction has weight and consequence, and where participation is the central point.

— Céline Tran, 2026
