Nail Art — Personal

Nail Designs

MediumBuilder Gel, Gel Polish, Plastic Nail Tips, UV Lamp
Period2020 — Present
TypePersonal Project

Nail art I've created over the years. Because my mother is a nail technician, I grew up with the tools and used quarantine to seriously level up my technique. I start by filling and shaping, apply fake tips with nail glue, then build up gel colour and accessories before sealing with a UV-cured top coat. These are designs I made for myself, for the joy of it.
