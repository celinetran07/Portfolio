Is a Body a City?

MediumAdobe Premiere Pro
Year2024
TypeCreative Video

A creative video made in Adobe Premiere Pro exploring the question: is a body a city, or a city a body? The piece draws parallels between the systems, structures, and flows that define both the human body and urban space, circulation, boundaries, density, and the constant negotiation between order and chaos.
